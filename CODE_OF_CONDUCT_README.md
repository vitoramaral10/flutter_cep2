## 🎯 Purpose

Our Code of Conduct serves to:

1. **Create Safety** - Ensure all community members feel safe and respected
2. **Set Expectations** - Clarify what behavior is and isn't acceptable
3. **Provide Process** - Define how violations are reported and handled
4. **Build Trust** - Demonstrate commitment to a welcoming community

- ✅ Project events (online and offline)

## 🚨 Reporting Violations

### Process

**If you experience or witness a violation:**

1. **Assess Safety** - Ensure you're in a safe position
2. **Document** - Note details (what, when, who, context)
3. **Report** - Email vitor.amaral10@gmail.com
4. **Confidentiality** - Your report will be kept confidential
5. **Investigation** - The team will investigate and respond

### What to Include in Report

- **Description** - What happened?
- **When** - Date and time
- **Who** - Involved parties
- **Context** - Platform, channel, public/private
- **Evidence** - Screenshots, links, logs (if available)
- **Impact** - How did this affect you?
- **Contact** - Preferred way to reach you

### Confidentiality

All reports are:
- ✅ Handled confidentially
- ✅ Investigated fairly
- ✅ Responded to appropriately
- ✅ Protected from retaliation

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

### Possible Actions

Depending on severity and context:

1. **Warning** - For minor, first-time violations
2. **Temporary Ban** - For repeated or moderate violations
3. **Permanent Ban** - For severe or repeated violations

### Factors Considered

- Severity of violation
- Pattern of behavior
- Intent (if applicable)
- Context and circumstances
- Community impact
